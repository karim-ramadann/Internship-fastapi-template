ARCHITECTURE PRINCIPLES ACHIEVED
===============================================================================

   - Add only org-wide standards (naming, tagging)
   - No business logic in modules

✅ Business Logic in Root
   - Task definitions, routing, environment decisions
   - Application-specific configuration
